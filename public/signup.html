MISSION

Our Same Kitchen was founded on the belief that hunger is not a food issue. It is a logistics issue. The United States wastes 40% of all food produced each year. That is $161 billion in uneaten food at retailers and restaurants and lost opportunities to feed the 50 million Americans who are food insecure. Diverting only 15% of wasted food would be enough to cut that number in half. That's where we come in.

Our Same Kitchen is a non-profit food delivery service for food establishments such as restaurants, grocery stores and farms. With the help of our volunteers, surplus food from food establishments are picked up and delivered to food banks, families and individuals in need.
